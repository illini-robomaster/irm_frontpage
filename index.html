What is RoboMaster?

RoboMaster is an annual robotics competition that combines engineering innovation with intense tactical gameplay. Teams design and build multiple robots to compete in a high-tech battlefield environment.

Our Team Structure

Specialized divisions working toward one goal

Mechanical Engineering

Design and manufacture robot chassis, weapon systems, and mechanical components using CAD software and precision manufacturing.

Electrical Engineering

Develop control systems, power management, sensor integration, and electronic hardware for our competitive robots.

Software Development

Create autonomous algorithms, computer vision systems, and real-time control software for tactical robot operations.

Strategy & Operations

Analyze competition rules, develop tactical strategies, and coordinate team logistics for optimal performance.
