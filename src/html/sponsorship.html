WHY SPONSOR IRM

Illini RoboMaster is a registered student organization at UIUC dedicated to advancing robotics through innovation and competition. We seek partnerships to support our mission and showcase cutting-edge engineering talent.

Value of Sponsorship:

  • • Brand Visibility: Logos on robots, uniforms, and media
  • • Recruitment: Access to highly skilled engineers
  • • Innovation Showcase: Align with cutting-edge robotics
  • • Community Engagement: Recognition at events

Platinum ($5,000+)

  • Invitation to private team demos and networking events

Gold ($2,000+)

  • Regular logo placement on one or more robots
  • Designated products for the Team
  • Recognition in all public team events
  • Access to the resume book

Silver ($500+)

Bronze (up to $500)
