About ILLini RoboMaster

Pioneering the future of competitive robotics at the University of Illinois

Our Mission

ILLini RoboMaster is a competitive robotics team at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign dedicated to advancing robotics technology through the prestigious RoboMaster competition. We bring together passionate students from engineering, computer science, and business to create cutting-edge robots while fostering innovation, teamwork, and technical excellence.

What is RoboMaster?

The RoboMaster University Championship is the world's premier robotics competition, featuring intense 7v7 robot battles that combine cutting-edge engineering, strategic gameplay, and real-time combat. Teams must design, build, and operate multiple specialized robots in a fast-paced battlefield environment.
